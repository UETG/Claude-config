# Privacy Policy — Scholar-Pilot

_Last updated: 2026-06-30_

Scholar-Pilot ("the GPT") is a research assistant that helps users find, review, and
discuss academic papers. This page explains how it handles your data.

## What is processed
- The search queries and messages you send to the GPT.
- Any files you choose to upload, processed solely to fulfill your request.

## Third-party services
To find papers, the keywords from your request are sent to public scholarly search APIs:
- **OpenAlex** — https://openalex.org
- **Semantic Scholar** — https://www.semanticscholar.org

These services receive only the search terms needed to return results. They do not
receive your identity from this GPT.

## Data storage & sharing
Scholar-Pilot does **not** run its own server and does **not** store, sell, or share your
personal data. Your conversations are handled by the OpenAI ChatGPT platform under
OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

## Responsible use
The optional "local setup" guidance helps users run paper search on their own machine
using their own legitimate institutional access. Users are responsible for complying with
the terms of service and rate limits of the websites they access.
